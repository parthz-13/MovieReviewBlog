MOVIE OVERVIEW

SYNOPSIS

The story follows Akash (Ayushmann Khurrana), a blind pianist whose life takes an unexpected turn when he becomes embroiled in a murder. While pretending to be blind to enhance his music, Akash unwittingly witnesses the crime. What follows is a rollercoaster of suspense, black comedy, and unexpected twists as Akash tries to navigate through layers of deception, crime, and moral dilemmas. The film explores themes of greed, ambition, and survival, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats until the final scene.

REVIEW

Andhadhun was universally praised for its masterful storytelling, innovative script, and stellar performances. Ayushmann Khurrana's portrayal of a morally ambiguous character was hailed as a career-defining performance. Tabu's portrayal of Simi, a manipulative and complex character, received widespread acclaim. Sriram Raghavan's direction was applauded for its ability to blend suspense, humor, and unpredictability seamlessly. The film keeps the audience guessing throughout, with its layered plot and unexpected twists. The music by Amit Trivedi and the piano sequences enhance the narrative, making it an immersive experience.
